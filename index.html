About

The iMessage SMS Simulator is an Electron-inspired web application that brings iMessage-style conversations to your browser. It offers ad-free streaming with customizable voice narration, allowing you to watch your simulated chats with a lightweight and user-friendly interface. Learn more on our GitHub.

Features

  • Ad-Free Simulation: Generate message sequences with a background video without interruptions.
  • Customizable Voices: Choose from available system voices for received and sent messages.
  • Dynamic Avatar: Automatically generates an avatar based on the conversation name.
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices.
  • Easy to Use: Simple interface to add messages, select videos, and generate simulations.
